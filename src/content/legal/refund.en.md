# Refund Policy

**Last updated May 13, 2026**

Thank you for your purchase. We hope you are happy with your Noteflex subscription. However, if you are not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you may request a refund. Please see below for more information on our refund policy.

## REFUND ELIGIBILITY

Refund requests must be submitted within fourteen (14) days of the purchase date. Because Noteflex is a digital service, refund eligibility depends on whether Premium features and content have been substantially accessed or used during the subscription period.

## REFUND REQUEST PROCESS

To request a refund:

1. Email customer service at [contact@noteflex.app](mailto:contact@noteflex.app) within 14 days of your purchase to obtain a Refund Authorization (RA) number.
2. Include in your email:
   - Your account email address
   - Paddle transaction ID or proof of purchase
   - Reason for refund request
3. After receiving an RA number, our team will review your request and confirm eligibility within 10 business days.

## REFUND PROCESSING

After receiving your refund request and verifying eligibility, we will process your refund. Please allow at least ten (10) business days from the receipt of your request to process the refund. Refunds may take 1-2 billing cycles to appear on your credit card or PayPal statement, depending on your payment provider.

We will notify you by email when your refund has been processed.

## EXCEPTIONS

The following items cannot be refunded:

- Digital content or services that have already been accessed or used after purchase
- Subscription periods that have been partially or fully consumed
- Subscriptions where the user has accessed Premium features after the start of the subscription period

For defective or damaged services, or if you experience technical issues that prevent you from accessing the Services, please contact us at the contact details below to arrange a refund.

## DIGITAL SUBSCRIPTION SPECIFICS

**1. Right of withdrawal under Korean law:** Under the Korean Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (§17), customers in South Korea may request a refund within 7 days of purchase, provided that Premium features have not been substantially used.

**2. EU/UK 14-day cooling-off period:** Customers residing in the European Union or United Kingdom have the right to withdraw from the subscription within 14 days of purchase under EU Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU and UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013, provided Premium features have not been substantially accessed.

**3. Refund processing:** All refunds are processed through our payment provider, Paddle ([https://www.paddle.com](https://www.paddle.com)), and will be returned to the original payment method within 10 business days of approval.

**4. Annual subscription refunds:** For annual subscriptions, refunds requested after the cooling-off period may be granted on a pro-rated basis at our sole discretion, deducting the value of any Premium features already used.

**5. Cancellation vs. refund:** Cancelling a subscription stops future renewals but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current paid period. Refund requests must be submitted separately to [contact@noteflex.app](mailto:contact@noteflex.app).
